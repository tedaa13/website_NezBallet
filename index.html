NEZ Ballet SCHOOL OFFERS A COMPLETE PROGRAM FOR YOUNG DANCER

Natural, Elegant, and Zealous or abbreviated NEZ is a studio for ballet and dance courses in Bandung.


NEZ studio commenced operations on June 1,2010. Having a main studio at Taman Kopo Indah 3 blok A no 49.


Nez Studio teachs five type of classes, which is:
Classical Ballet (Most Popular)
Jazz
Modern Dance
Hip-Hop
Traditional Dance


NEZ Studio is a private company with Ms. Agnes Theda S.Sn as a single owner.
